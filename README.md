To use this Windows Form Application, You just need to type your Income on the **Income text box** 
 and your Tax Rate on percentage (%) on the **Tax Rate text box**.


![Screenshot 2023-09-24 225145](https://github.com/alexdones24/TaxCalculator/assets/145957406/51f8718e-2682-4cd2-8450-873122dd8c1f)
![Screenshot 2023-09-24 225227](https://github.com/alexdones24/TaxCalculator/assets/145957406/d7861a71-90e5-4b47-af46-da674af6403b)


After that you input your data, click the **calculate tax button** and your datas will appear on the DataGridView. 

![Screenshot 2023-09-24 225522](https://github.com/alexdones24/TaxCalculator/assets/145957406/efaf4188-e196-4e70-b68e-8b821de406d1)

![Screenshot 2023-09-24 225317](https://github.com/alexdones24/TaxCalculator/assets/145957406/5491c19e-d0d7-43bc-a0bd-18ec8e4d1307)

However, you can only input numerical values on the text box and not alphabet characters as it will ask you to enter another value.
![Screenshot 2023-09-24 225351](https://github.com/alexdones24/TaxCalculator/assets/145957406/4fc1f97f-6d4f-43bc-a1ed-f52c46579c53)

To reset your data, you just need to click the **reset button** and to close the application, you just need to click the **Exit button**.
![Screenshot 2023-09-24 225432](https://github.com/alexdones24/TaxCalculator/assets/145957406/61a1df90-fa3c-48d3-a981-44b46fe6f423)
